5. Cookies

We use cookies to make the Service work and to understand how it is used. When you first visit the site, a cookie consent banner will appear asking for your preferences.

Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the site to function — keeping you logged in and remembering your session. They cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site in aggregate. These are only placed if you click "Accept" on the cookie banner. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

Payment Cookies

Our payment providers (Mollie, Stripe) may set cookies for fraud prevention and payment processing.

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to log in and use the Service.

We use the following third-party services who may process your data:

Each service has its own privacy policy and operates under appropriate data protection agreements.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active, plus up to 2 years after your last login. Financial records are kept for 6 years as required by HMRC. You may request deletion at any time (subject to legal retention obligations).

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

Access

Request a copy of your personal data

Rectification

Ask us to correct inaccurate data

Erasure

Request deletion of your account and all associated data

Portability

Receive your data in a portable format

Objection

Object to processing based on legitimate interests

Withdraw Consent

Opt out of marketing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@passr0exams.com. We will respond within 30 days.

For erasure requests, we will delete your profile, all study progress records, and quiz history. Your Supabase authentication account will also be removed. Note that financial transaction records are retained for 6 years as required by HMRC.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8a. Consent at Registration

When creating an account, you are required to actively confirm that you have read and agreed to these policies via a checkbox on the registration form. This constitutes your consent under UK GDPR for us to process your personal data as described in this policy. You may withdraw consent at any time by requesting account deletion.

9. Data Security

10. Changes to This Policy
