Why I built this

After passing R01, I found myself frustrated with the available study options. Textbooks are dense. Past paper PDFs are static. Most revision sites feel like they were built for the syllabus, not for the person trying to pass in six weeks while working full time.

I also have a genuine interest in markets and investing — I actively manage my own Stocks & Shares ISA and Lifetime ISA across growth equities and macro themes. When I write about investment principles, portfolio construction, or risk, I'm drawing on real understanding, not just textbook definitions. That perspective makes a difference when you're trying to actually understand the material rather than memorise it.

PassDipPFS exists because I wanted a smarter way to prepare — and I had the skills to build it.
